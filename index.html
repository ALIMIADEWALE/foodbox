Who we are.

FoodboxNG is a global technology enabled food retail/distribution company via aggregation of farm produce at farm gates and last mile distribution to customers/clients in Nigeria and diaspora.
Foodbox.ng is a bold and inspiring startup committed to improving the lives of farmers in rural communities and consumers in urban city hubs through technology-enabled channels and a strong last mile distribution network
